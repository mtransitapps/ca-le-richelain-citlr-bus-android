This application adds exo Le Richelain / Roussillon buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the planned schedule as well as real time service statuses and the latest news from exo.quebec, @allo_exo and @exo_Sud on Twitter.

exo Le Richelain / Roussillon serves Delson, Saint-Constant, Sainte-Catherine, Brossard, Candiac, La Prairie, Longueuil, Montreal and Saint-Philippe.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by exo.
https://exo.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-le-richelain-citlr-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Le Richelain / Roussillon.

exo Le Richelain / Roussillon was formerly known as RTM Le Richelain / CIT Roussillon sector and CIT Le Richelain / CIT Roussillon.
exo was formerly known as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time service statuses and to read news from exo.quebec and Twitter
